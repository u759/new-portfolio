# Ayden Nguyen

Student from UBC Vancouver with a love for aviation and software. In 2022, I got my private pilot's license, joining UBC AeroDesign in 2023 to build an autonomous RC aircraft. After achieving the team's highest ever thrust-to-weight ratio in 2024, I joined the firmware team to design autonomous flight firmware on an STM32. In 2026, we achieved 1st-place flight score against 20+ universities worldwide. I am focused on improving the reliability, maintainability, and performance of our code. We aim to develop the best RC flight firmware in the world.

I also love building software. I am the co-founder of mealSense, a mobile app designed to track macro-intake, nearby restaurant/grocery locations, and help prepare meals with an interactive AI voice agent. It is built on Flutter, Java Spring Boot, MongoDB, Cloudflare R2, Firebase SDK, Gemini AI, and an Oracle VPS.

I am currently working on PromptCodr, a platform to practice AI-prompting with quantitative feedback. In the age of AI, the bottleneck is shifting from coding ability to system design - and without the right prompt, you waste tokens generating content that does the wrong thing. My goal is to help engineers and businesses improve productivity and save costs by training prompt-engineering skills. PromptCodr evaluates you based on token count, cost, latency, and correctedness, helping engineers develop effective prompting techniques. Using less tokens also saves trees, so everyone wins.
